Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Healthy people aged 18–65 years and weighing at least 50 kg can donate blood.

How often can I donate blood?

Every 3 months if you are healthy.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes. All equipment used is sterile and used only once.
